THE OTHERWORLDLY AURORA borealis, or northrn lights, begin high in the Earth's atmosphere---at altitudes from 60 to more than 250 miles---when charged particles from the sun become trapped in the Earth's magnetic field. The result is a colorful, dancing light show.


Glacier Experience is a small group that offers an easy glacier hike on Sólheimajökull glacier with an experienced glacier guide and is available all year around. The tour offers a pick-up from Reykjavik or a meet on location at Sólheimajökull glacier. Add even more adventure to your stay in Iceland and come explore this amazing crawling glacier with us.

Sólheimajökull glacier is an outlet from the mighty Mýrdalsjökull and is surrounded by some of Iceland's most grand volcanoes and during summertime, a glacier lagoon forms in front of the glacier only adding to the charm of the location. This popular day tour is the perfect way to get in real contact with the true reason behind the name Ice-land!
